What we offer
for your best holidays in Saranda

Our apartment boasts a prime location with easy access to several attractions and amenities. Explore Butrint National Park just 3 km away. Relax at Saranda City Beach (100 m) or other nearby beaches. The beautiful Ksamil Islands, including Islet of Ksamil, Islet of Rrëza, and Islet of St. George, are also within a short drive. Plus, travel is convenient with Corfu International Airport 30 km away and Ioannina Airport 71 km away. This central location offers both comfort and accessibility to the best of the area.

Our team at Saranda City Center Apartment offers motorcycle rentals.
We also provide taxi services including transfers from Nënë Tereza Airport to Saranda, from Saranda to Nënë Tereza Airport, and various other routes, such as Saranda to Vlorë.

Our apartment offers a great view for photography, with a balcony providing a full 180° (180-degree) sea view.

If you're working remotely, internet speed won't be an issue because the apartment in Saranda City is equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi offering download speeds of 300 Mbps+. This ensures you have the convenience and reliability you need.
